Student Representative

One aspect of our mission as the Roosevelt High School Alumni Association is to support the current students of Roosevelt High School. In order to best do this, student voice is imperative.

The organization will be electing up to two current students to present a student voice towards our ongoing work. These students will serve a term of one school year.

Applicants must be a current RHS junior or senior and have a minimum 2.5 GPA.

Applications are due on November 1st, 2024. The following application must be completed in entirety.
