Grants

One aspect of our mission as the Roosevelt High School Alumni Association is to support the students and staff of Roosevelt High School. Part of how we do that is by providing grants towards projects that students are passionate about.
Fill out one of our online grant application form linked below and your request will be submitted to the Executive Committee for review.

Small Grant Request Form

Special Grant Request Form
Our Small Grant program supports awards up to $250 and is awarded frequently. We encourage students to apply on behalf of their organizations with a staff member as a co-signer on the request.

For larger awards, our Special Grant program allows us to work closely with the requesting organization to fully understand their needs and find how we can best support their project. These requests must be submitted by a staff member and may have students as co-signers. These grant requests require meeting with an RHSAA representative to discuss details before the grant is awarded.

Grant requests are accepted on a rolling basis. Please email executive@rooseveltalumni.org with any questions.
