Fred Meyer Rewards

Using your Fred Meyer Rewards Card as you normally do, you can support our organization by linking your card to our nonprofit. Follow these steps to set up this process, and whenever you use your card you will earn your same rewards, while also sending us a reward!

Click on this box to follow the link to access Fred Meyer Rewards.
Once you have logged-in to your Fred Meyer Rewards account, you can link your Rewards Card to the RHSAA by selecting the "Community Rewards" option under the "Rewards" tab. Follow the instructions and enter the RHSAA's Organization Number: YU141.
Then, shop at your neighborhood Fred Meyer and know that every purchase you make will benefit our efforts to strengthen our community of alumni and ultimately our Alma Mater, our Roosevelt High School.


Here are some examples of how your donations have helped recently!

PTSA

Our grant to the PTSA helped them make their final fundraising goal for all-night graduation party. By supporting this party, these graduates were able to have a fun (and safe) celebration following their graduation.

Football

Our grant to the football team sent seven varsity players to Seaside for a tournament. The grant covered registration, food, and lodging. The team won 6 of their 7 games!

Library

Our grant to the library helped fund a poetry slam competition with refreshments and other needed items for the event. Two of the poets went on to compete in the city wide poetry slam, Verselandia, and did excellent!
