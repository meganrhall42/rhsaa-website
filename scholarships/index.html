Scholarships

The Roosevelt High School Alumni Association is excited to offer five $1,000 college scholarships to the Class of 2024. Applications are due on April 1st, 2024. The information below details the application and selection process.

2024 RECIPIENTS HAVE BEEN AWARDED

If you have questions, please email the committee at scholarships@rooseveltalumni.org and we will get back to you!

Applications for the Class of 2025 will open in February 2025.

Selection

The RHSAA has selected a committee of five alumni who will award this scholarship. The committee will complete a blind review of each application (identifying information will be removed from materials) and choose five winners based on both academic and personal achievement. In the event that additional information is required, students will be contacted via email and given 7 days to respond.

Questions?

Email the committee at scholarships@rooseveltalumni.org and we will get back to you!
