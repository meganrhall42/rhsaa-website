Scholarships

The Roosevelt High School Alumni Association is excited to offer three $1,000 college scholarships to the Class of 2023. Applications are due on April 3rd, 2023. The information below details the application and selection process.

Qualifications

Each applicant must meet the following:
  • Current Roosevelt High School student
  • Senior, graduating with the Class of 2023
  • Minimum 3.0 cumulative GPA, unweighted

Application

Each applicant must complete the following:
  • Two 250 word essays of the following prompts (taken from the OSAC application):
    1. What have you done for your family, school, or community that you care about the most and why?
    2. Describe a personal accomplishment, impactful change or experience that has occurred in your life. What skills and strengths were needed to respond, and what did you learn about yourself?
  • A single-sided, one page resume that includes GPA; other resume elements may include Community Service, Extracurricular Activities, Work Experience, Honors/Awards etc.
    1. Note: all personal identifying information will be blacked out before being turned over to the committee; items such as references or contact information can be excluded completely to accommodate other details.
  • Unofficial high school transcript.
    1. Note: unofficial transcript will only be used to confirm the three qualification points are met and are not turned over to the review committee.

Submission

Below is a link to our online application. A google account is required to be able to upload your files and submit. Please read over your application fully before submitting as editing will not be allowed once the application is submitted.

Once an application is submitted, an automated email will be sent to the applicant with a copy of their submission. Within 24 hours, a member of the committee will do a manual review to confirm the applicant meets qualifications and has included all required parts of the application. The reviewer will then email the applicant to confirm receipt of the application, or ask for additional information if required. Please contact the email address below if the applicant does not receive confirmation.

Please email scholarships@rooseveltalumni.org if you have any questions.

Selection

The RHSAA has selected a committee of five alumni who will award this scholarship. The committee will complete a blind review of each application (identifying information will be removed from materials) and choose three winners based on both academic and personal achievement. In the event that additional information is required, students will be contacted via email and given 7 days to respond.

Award

The three students who are selected as winners will be notified via email by May 29th, 2023.

The students will be presented with their award during the Senior Award Assembly before graduation.

Award recipients will be asked for permission to share a photo and a short summary of accomplishments and future plans with members of the RHSAA.

Questions?

Email the committee at scholarships@rooseveltalumni.org and we will get back to you!
