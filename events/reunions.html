Reunions

Your high school reunion is a great time to reconnect with those you grew up with. To learn where people led their lives, the interesting achievements everyone has made, and have a laugh over fond memories. Many of us may have Facebook now, but nothing beats getting together in person and sharing a smile face to face.

Class of 1963

Saturday, June 17, 2023 | 4:00-7:00pm
Twilight Room, 5242 N Lombard Street

Greetings Fellow Roughriders, Class of 1963! The Class of 1963 is planning a 60th reunion on Saturday, June 17, 2023, at the Twilight Room, 5242 N Lombard Street, from 4:00-7:00. It will be a ‘drop-in, come-as-you-are’ event. There will be a no-host bar and food service. Please reply to babyrh4345@gmail.com by May 30th if you plan to attend or need any additional information.

Class of 1973

July 14th & 15th, 2023

Our 50th Reunion is free for members of the class of 1973! On Friday night, July 14, 2023, join us at the Eagles for a night of great food, music, dance, drinks, memories and much more! Prior to the Reunion, there will be a guided tour of the $100 million remodeled Roosevelt High School. On Saturday, July 15, 2023, come to the Alumni Association's All-Class Picnic at Columbia Park from Noon to 4:00 pm. Following the picnic, a fun karaoke event will be held at the Eagles. Please see flyers below for all the details and how to register.
