Reunions

Your high school reunion is a great time to reconnect with those you grew up with. To learn where people led their lives, the interesting achievements everyone has made, and have a laugh over fond memories. Many of us may have Facebook now, but nothing beats getting together in person and sharing a smile face to face.
IMPORTANT NOTE: Due to COVID-19, many reunions are canceled or postponed. Reunions may be scheduled for a later date, check with your class reunion committee for more information.
If you know the status of your reunion and it is not listed here, please contact our webmaster to get an update posted by emailing: webmaster@rooseveltalumni.org.
