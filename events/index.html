Centennial Homecoming

Friday, September 29th, 2023
Roosevelt High School

We are excited to celebrate 100 years of the current campus of Roosevelt High School! September 29th will be a big day of festivities and all students, staff, community members, and alumni are invited to join us.

Here is the timeline of events:

4:00pm - Girls Soccer vs Grant

4:30pm - Tailgate BBQ & Activities

5:00pm - School Tours

5:30pm - Community Booths

7:00pm - Football vs Cleveland

Start the afternoon off supporting Varsity Girls Soccer as they take on Grant. These athletes have had a great start to their season, and a large home crowd is sure to work to their advantage.

The Roosevelt Supporter Club and RHS Alumni Association have partnered together to offer a free barbeque dinner to students and the community. Come to the roundabout in the parking lot to get a meal starting at 4:30pm, food will be served until we run out. Take your plate and head to the front lawn where student leadership will be running games and activities.

Check out the fully remodeled campus and get a tour of the school from student-alumni guide duos. Meet at the entrance to the gymnasium where tours will depart at 5:00pm, 5:30pm, and 6:00pm.

Browse the booths set-up at the community fair outside the football field starting at 5:30pm, they will be open until the game. The Alumni Association, student groups, and community organizations will be tabling with information, centennial souvenirs, and more.

The big Football game begins at 7:00pm, be sure to find your seat in the stands! The band, cheer squad, and dance team will be there to hype up the crowd as we take on Cleveland. Tickets are $6 for adults and $2 for students at the gate.

We are excited for this big community event as we celebrate our century of history!
