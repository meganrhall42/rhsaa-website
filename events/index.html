It's time: 2023 marks the 100th anniversary of our current campus, and we are excited to bring you a year of celebratory events! As more events come to fruition we will update this page.

Centennial Track Meet

Wednesday, May 3rd, 2023 - 3:30pm - 7:30pm
Roosevelt High School Track

All alumni, community members, staff, and students are invited to come pack the stands to celebrate 100 years of history with Roosevelt Athletics! Drop-in anytime in the evening and watch some impressive feats of athleticism. Admission is free.

Centennial Music Night

Wednesday, May 24th, 2023 - 7:00pm
Roosevelt High School Auditorium

All alumni, community members, staff, and students are invited to enjoy the talented variety of student musicians as a celebration of 100 years of history with Roosevelt Music! The award winning band has a packed show for us at this special one night event. Admission is free.

Centennial All-Class Picnic

Saturday, July 15th, 2023 - 12:00pm - 4:00pm
Columbia Park

Our usual all-class picnic, with an extra exciting Centennial twist! Alumni of all classes are invited to join this special celebration for all ages. Planning is still in the works and more details will come as we get closer to summer.
