64th Annual Golf Tournament

Saturday, September 7th, 2024
Club Green Meadows - Vancouver, WA

Our friends at the Roosevelt Booster Club are proud to present their annual golf tournament and fundraiser. They are a 501(c)(3) non-profit, volunteer-run organization working to supplement and support student programs by providing financial assistance to extracurricular clubs, the arts, and athletics. A great time to socialize and help a good cause!

Golf registration begins at 11:30am, and 1:00pm shotgun start. Catered dinner begins at 6:00pm. Download the registration form below.
