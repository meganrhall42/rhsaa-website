Centennial Choir Concert

Thursday, December 14th, 2023 - 7:00pm
Roosevelt High School Auditorium

We are closing out our Centennial with one last celebration in honor of 100 years of the current campus of Roosevelt High School!

Students, staff, community members, and alumni are invited to join us for a beautiful night of music presented by our award winning choir students. There will also be a guest performance from the George Middle School choir, a new program we are excited to watch grow.

Admission is free, and we will be accepting donations to support these students. We will also be selling Centennial souvenirs that include t-shirts, a history book, and more.

We are also collecting gift cards to contribute to the holiday drive that benefits RHS students and families in need. We are looking for small gift cards that are aimed at teens. All items collected will be given to families in late December.



Please note that the date has changed from the original notice posted. December 12th is now the Orchestra Concert, if you aren't able to attend the Choir Concert due to the change, support these other music students!
