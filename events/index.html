2024 Homecoming

Friday, October 4th, 2024
Roosevelt High School

Join us for a community celebration of Roosevelt High School!

Free Community BBQ: We'll be serving meals from 4:30pm up to game time, bring your family and grab a dish. Find us in the parking lot turn-around on the west side of campus. Hosted by the Alumni Association and the Roosevelt Supporter Club

Tailgate Activities: Check out games, booths, and other activities. Located on the softball field and into the parking lot. Hosted by student leadership.

Football vs. Lincoln: Pack the stands for what is sure to be a great game, padded with performances by Band, Cheer, and Dance. Enter through the game in the parking lot.

Football tickets are $7 for adults, $5 for students and senior citizens. Concessions will be available for purchase, funds support a variety of student clubs.

2025 All-Class Picnic

Save the date for the 2025 All-Class Picnic! This is a casual potluck in the park that alumni of all ages are invited to. Planning begins in February 2025, stay tuned for more details.
