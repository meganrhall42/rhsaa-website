It's time: 2023 marks the 100th anniversary of our current campus, and we are excited to bring you a year of celebratory events! As more events come to fruition we will update this page.

Centennial All-Class Picnic

Saturday, July 15th, 2023 - 12:00pm - 4:00pm
Columbia Park | Site E

Our usual all-class picnic, with an extra exciting Centennial twist! Alumni of all classes are invited to join this special celebration for all ages. Bring a drink, a chair, and a dish to share. Hotdogs and tableware provided. Drop in any time and visit with classmates.

63rd Annual Golf Tournament

Saturday, August 26th, 2023
Club Green Meadows - Vancouver, WA

Our friends at the Roosevelt Booster Club are proud to present their annual golf tournament and fundraiser. They are a 501(c)(3) non-profit, volunteer-run organization working to supplement and support student programs by providing financial assistance to extracurricular clubs, the arts, and athletics. A great time to socialize and help a good cause!

Golf registration begins at 11:30am, and 1:00pm shotgun start. Dinner catered by Big Kahuna begins at 6:00pm. Purchase tickets at the link below.
